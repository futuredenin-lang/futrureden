Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At FUTUREDEN, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our capsule housing services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number
  • Inquiry details submitted through contact or booking forms
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries about our Cruise, Studio, and Tokyo capsule models
  • To provide quotations, updates, and customer support
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • For internal analytics and business development

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

FUTUREDEN does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only with trusted partners for operational purposes, under strict confidentiality.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Policy Updates

FUTUREDEN may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at futureden.in@gmail.com.
